FAQ

First time at ATB?

Here are the big things to know before you pull up.

What is All Team Bash?

All Team Bash is an automotive lifestyle event hosted by Kimo & Maria. It was created to bring teams, enthusiasts, and creative individuals together without car judges or awards based on builds.

Is the event family friendly?

Yes. ATB events are family friendly and geared toward enthusiasts and their families.

Does it cost to attend?

Entry is typically $20 at the gate per person and $15 online during presale. Children 10 and under get in free. Military and first responder discounts may apply with valid ID.

Where can I buy tickets?

Presale ticket links are usually posted before each event on the website schedule section and on the Facebook event page. You can also pay at the gate.

What time does the event run?

Events are generally open to the public from 9 AM to 2 PM. Check the schedule page for the specific event details.

Can I attend if I am not part of a team?

Yes. You do not have to be part of a team or car club to attend. Everyone who supports the scene is welcome.

Can I bring my dog?

Yes, dogs are welcome. They must remain on a leash, and guests are asked to clean up after them.

Can I bring food or drinks?

Yes. Food, BBQ, and ice chests are permitted. Please keep your area clean and make sure charcoal is completely cooled before disposal.

Will there be food trucks and vendors?

Yes. ATB works to bring a variety of food trucks, local vendors, and small businesses to the events.

How do I become a vendor?

Email allteambash@gmail.com with the event you are interested in and a description of your business. Vendor pricing and approval may vary by event.

Does ATB sell merchandise?

Yes. ATB sells merchandise at events, including shirts, hats, decals, lanyards, and other items.

Are there trophy categories?

ATB does not judge cars for trophies. They do offer game trophies and a specialty King of Clubs trophy that is passed from team to team.

How can I stay connected?

Follow All Team Bash on Instagram and Facebook, and check the website schedule for upcoming events.

Still have questions?

Reach out anytime.

If your question was not answered here, send the ATB team a message and they will help you get pointed in the right direction.
