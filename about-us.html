Our story

From park meetup to movement.

What started as a passion project became something bigger: a place where different teams, styles, builds, and backgrounds could come together under one shared love for the automotive lifestyle.

It was never supposed to be just a business.

All Team Bash is hosted by Kimo & Maria, alongside a close circle of friends who have helped shape the event from the beginning.

After many years in the car scene with their own club, Team Sunworks, they created All Team Bash to bring people together in a different way — not around car judges or awards, but around fellowship, respect, and the joy of showing up for one another.

The first All Team Bash was held at Bear Creek Park in Houston, Texas in June 2008. By 2012, the gathering had outgrown the park. In 2013, the event moved into its first rented venue, and over the next five years, it reached four different states.

That growth was unexpected. The heart behind it was not.
